Discover the wonders of Angkor Wat, Khmer culture, and Cambodia’s hidden gems.

Temples

Bayon Temples

Visit the iconic Bayon Temple in the heart of Angkor Thom, famous for its massive stone faces carved into towers.

Explore the intricate bas-reliefs depicting Khmer history, mythology, and everyday life, making it a photographer’s paradise.

Nature

Khnang Phsa

Explore the peaceful Khnang Phsa area with its rolling hills, dense forests, and scenic rivers, ideal for hiking and nature walks.

Spot local wildlife, enjoy birdwatching, and immerse yourself in the serenity of Cambodia’s countryside.

City

Phnom Penh City

Discover Phnom Penh, Cambodia’s vibrant capital, with its lively markets, riverside promenade, and historical landmarks.

Visit the Royal Palace, National Museum, and local street food spots to experience the city’s rich culture and modern energy.

Nature

Kulen Waterfalls

Visit Kulen Waterfalls, a stunning natural landmark surrounded by lush forest and sacred sites like the reclining Buddha.

Swim in the crystal-clear pools, enjoy the cascading waters, and explore the scenic trails nearby for a perfect nature retreat.
